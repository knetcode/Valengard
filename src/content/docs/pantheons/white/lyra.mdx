- **Titles**: The Hand of Mercy, The Great Comforter, The Giver of Redemption, The Light in the Gloom
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Life, Light, Healing
- **Description**: Lyra represents compassion, forgiveness, and selfless healing, focusing on aiding the poor, sick, and downtrodden. She encourages acts of charity, community support, and finding redemption, believing that no soul is entirely beyond hope. Lyra's presence provides solace and reminds mortals that kindness and altruism are the greatest virtues. She urges her followers to act not for reward, but purely out of love for fellow sentient beings.
- **Worshippers**: Healers, nurses, caregivers, charitable workers, nuns and priests dedicated to the needy, redemption-seeking criminals, and those who maintain hospices and shelters. They honor Lyra by donating their time and resources to the less fortunate, practicing forgiveness, and never turning away someone in genuine need.
- **Passive Boon**: Healing Hand - You gain profiency in Medicine skill checks and know the Spare The Dying cantrip
- **Active Boon**: Hand of Mercy - Once per long rest, whenever an ally you can see is healed, you can spend your reaction to grant them temporary hit points equal to your character level.
