- **Titles**: The Just, Father of Authority, The Impartial Scale, The Enforcer of Equity
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Domains**: Peace, Order, Authority
- **Description**: Auriel is the embodiment of impartial justice and civil order. His followers strive to establish equitable laws, maintain stability in society, and protect the innocent through disciplined authority. He represents the ideal of a perfectly just society where laws are fair, enforcement is unwavering, and citizens live in peaceful security. Auriel despises tyranny and anarchy equally, demanding structured goodness.
- **Worshippers**: Judges, city guards, magistrates, military commanders who uphold codes of honor, paladins dedicated to justice, and lawmakers. They honor Auriel through strict adherence to the law, protecting the weakest members of society, and participating in the fair governance of their communities.
- **Passive Boon**: Justice - You have advantage whenever performing a Lawful Good Charmisa, Persuasion or Intimidation skill check.
- **Active Boon**: Authority - Once per long rest, you can spend your reaction when a creature targets one your your allies with an attack action, they must make a DC18 Wisdom Saving Throw, if they fail they immediately end their turn.
