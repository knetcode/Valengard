- **Titles**: The Unblemished Star, The Light of Divine Duty, The Watcher Against Corruption, The Immaculate Barrier
- **Alignment**: Lawful Good
- **Domains**: Light, Order, Sanctuary
- **Description**: Seraphine is the deity of absolute purity, divine duty, and eternal vigilance. She demands strict adherence to vows and moral codes, acting as a barrier against corruption and the slow creep of evil. She represents light used as a tool of cleansing and judgment, favoring those who maintain unblemished integrity. Seraphine is often worshiped by monastic orders and keepers of sacred relics. She judges all beings based on their loyalty to their sworn oaths and the purity of their intent.
- **Worshippers**: Monastic orders, keepers of sacred relics, priests who administer oaths, paladins and clerics who take binding vows, sentinels, and any who dedicate their lives to resisting personal or societal corruption. They honor Seraphine by performing cleansing rituals, meticulously following established sacred routines, and confessing and atoning for any broken vow.
- **Passive Boon**: Unblemished Star - You gain resistance to Necrotic damage.
- **Active Boon**: Divine Light - Once per long rest, you can use your action to cast Faerie Fire. When cast this way, the saving throw is DC18 Wisdom and allies can automatically succeed.
