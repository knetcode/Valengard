- **Titles**: The Shield of Valengard, The Bulwark of the Righteous, The Unbreakable Guard, The Master Strategist
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: War, Protection, Military
- **Description**: Paximus is the patron of disciplined soldiers, vigilant guards, and tactical defense. He is the god of strategic defense, valor, and the unwavering commitment to one's home and people, protecting society's righteous structure. Paximus despises aggressive warfare and cruelty, teaching that true strength is found in discipline, coordinated defense, and sacrificing for the good of the many. His symbol is the perfectly forged shield.
- **Worshippers**: Soldiers, city guards, military strategists, Paladins dedicated to defense, fortification builders, and those who stand as a bulwark against evil. They honor Paximus through rigorous training, upholding strict codes of conduct, and maintaining eternal vigilance at their posts.
- **Passive Boon**: Bulwark Shield - You gain a permanent +1 to AC
- **Active Boon**: Unwavering Protector - Once per long rest, you can use your reaction to cast the spell Shield on yourself or any ally you can see.
