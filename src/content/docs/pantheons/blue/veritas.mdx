- **Titles**: The Prime Sage, The Axiomatic Truth, The Master of Flawless Logic, The Unclouded Mind
- **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral
- **Domains**: Knowledge, Order, Insight
- **Description**: Veritas is the deity of pure, axiomatic logic, deductive reasoning, and philosophical systems. This god is concerned with the integrity of thought and the construction of theories and proofs, viewing objective truth as a goal to be reached through flawless mental discipline. Worship involves meditation, the creation of logical treatises, and the rigorous testing of hypotheses. Veritas despises emotional bias, fallacy, and any form of deceit that clouds the pursuit of objective reality.
- **Worshippers**: Judges, magistrates, master detectives, logicians, philosophers, monastic scholars, high-ranking military strategists, and anyone who builds intricate, lawful systems of thought or governance. They venerate Veritas through debate, the preservation of logical integrity, and by always adhering to the clear, discernible facts of any situation.
- **Passive Boon**: Truth Sage - You gain expertise in the Insight skill
- **Active Boon**: Unclouded Mind - Once per day as a free action, you impose cool logic to force none of your rolls to suffer from disadvantage for one minute.
