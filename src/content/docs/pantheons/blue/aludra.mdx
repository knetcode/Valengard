- **Titles**: The Arch-Scribe, The Keeper of the Infinite Archive, The Unblinking Eye of History
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Knowledge, History, Scholars
- **Description**: Aludra is the patron of record-keepers, librarians, and the physical act of documentation. Her focus is the unbiased preservation of every scrap of historical, cultural, or magical knowledge. Her followers are dedicated to copying, indexing, and protecting archives from destruction and corruption, believing that the truth lies only within a complete record. She is indifferent to the morality of the information itself, valuing only its existence.
- **Worshippers**: Librarians, chroniclers, historians, scholars, monks who spend their lives copying ancient texts, archivists, and any who dedicate themselves to the collection and protection of lore. They honor Aludra with quiet study, meticulous organization, and by ensuring the secure storage of information.
- **Passive Boon**: Historian - You gain expertise in History skill checks.
- **Active Boon**: Infinite Archive - Once per long rest, you can use your bonus action to add a +5 to all Intelligence, Arcana, Investigation, Nature, Religion, History skill checks and Advantage on Intelligence saving throws for 1 minute
