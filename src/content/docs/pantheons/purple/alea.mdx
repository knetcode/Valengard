- **Titles**: The Bringer of Fortune, The Smiling Die, The Lady of the Random Road, The Thrill of Chance
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Good
- **Domains**: Luck, Travel, Chance
- **Description**: Alea represents random chance, fortunate accidents, and the thrill of the unexpected twist of fate. This god is worshipped by gamblers, travelers, and adventurers who rely on a sudden turn of luck to survive or succeed. Alea embodies the idea that destiny is not fixed, but subject to random, benevolent chaos. She encourages her followers to take risks, trust their gut, and embrace the unpredictability of life, knowing that fortune favors the bold.
- **Worshippers**: Gamblers, adventurers, rogues with a lucky streak, street urchins, caravan drivers, explorers, and anyone who thrives on chance and quick thinking. They honor Alea through wagers, games of chance, random acts of kindness, and by never taking unnecessary precautions, trusting in her capricious favor.
- **Passive Boon**: Gamblers Thrill - Whenever you roll a 2 on a d20 check, you can choose to reroll the die, you must use the new roll.
- **Active Boon**: Bringer of Fortune - Once per long rest, whenever you or any ally you can see spends their Heroic Inspiration, you can force this roll to be a natural 20.
