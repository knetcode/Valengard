- **Titles**: The Silver Hand, The Merchant's Balance, The Patron of the Coin, The Master of the Supply Chain
- **Alignment**: Lawful Neutral
- **Domains**: Trade, Travel, Commerce
- **Description**: Argentum is the deity of merchants, trade routes, and the profitable exchange of value. This god ensures that market transactions are conducted with neutrality and precision, valuing the skillful art of buying low and selling high. Followers are dedicated to organizing successful caravans, mastering supply chains, and establishing the vast commercial networks that make the city prosperous. Argentum embodies the efficiency of lawful, organized markets and abhors theft, fraud, and any disruption to the flow of goods.
- **Worshippers**: Merchants, guild masters, bankers, caravan guards, brokers, customs agents, city councilors focused on economics, and anyone who profits from well-organized commerce. They honor Argentum through honest, efficient deals, meticulous accounting, and ensuring the safety of trade convoys.
- **Passive Boon**: Merchant's tongue - You gain a +5 to any trade negotion skill checks that use a Persuasion skill check.
- **Active Boon**: Silver Hand - Once per long rest, you can use your reaction to add an addtional 1d10 to any Persuasion check. You can do this after you have rolled the intial check.
