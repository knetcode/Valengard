- **Titles**: The Artisan of Iron, The Master of the Hammer, The Soul of the Forge, The Maker of Perfect Tools
- **Alignment**: Neutral
- **Domains**: Forge, Artifice, Trade
- **Description**: Volund is the patron of skilled smiths, innovative mechanics, and metalworkers. This god is concerned with the raw talent of the craftsman, the quality of the tool, and the efficient creation of goods for trade. Volund's followers seek perfection in the physical labor of the forge and the workshop, believing that true divinity is found in the meticulous process of creation and the enduring excellence of the finished product. He cares only for skill, regardless of the item's purpose.
- **Worshippers**: Blacksmiths, jewelers, weaponsmiths, tinkers, clockmakers, inventors, and any artisan who relies on skill and patience to craft complex or essential items. They honor Volund by maintaining pristine workshops, never rushing a job, and always seeking to surpass their previous quality of work.
- **Passive Boon**: Smith's Hand - You start with Smith's tools and gain expertise in them.
- **Active Boon**: Artisan of Iron - Once per long rest, you can spend 10 minutes to sharpen a metal weapon or reinforce a metal armor. This grants a +2 to hit and damage for weapons or a +2 to AC for armour. This bonus will last the next 5 rounds of combat.
