- **Titles**: The Deep Scourer, The Guardian of the Dark Vein, The Gem-Father, The Honored Prospector
- **Alignment**: Neutral Good
- **Domains**: Geology, Protection, Earth
- **Description**: Kaldor is the deity of safe mining, geological abundance, and the retrieval of raw, precious materials. His followers are miners, prospectors, and gem-cutters who work deep beneath the earth. Kaldor values the careful extraction of resources and the protection of those who brave the dark for wealth, ensuring that tunnels hold true and veins run deep. He advocates for respecting the earth and the dangers it holds, demanding proper safety and planning for every descent.
- **Worshippers**: Miners, prospectors, metallurgists, gem-cutters, cavern explorers, engineers specializing in shoring and tunneling, and dwarven clans. They honor Kaldor by always leaving a small offering of metal or stone before an excavation, maintaining safety protocols, and sharing the bounty of a large find.
- **Passive Boon**: Deep Worker - You gain advantage when making any perception or investigation check in natural caves, underground caverns or geological hazards.
- **Active Boon**: Rock Crusher - Once per long rest, you can use your bonus action to add 3d8 bludgeoning damage to your successful weapon attack.
