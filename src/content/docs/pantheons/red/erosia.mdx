- **Titles**: The Flame of Passion, The Heart's Desire, The Unrestrained Muse, The Giver of Vitality
- **Alignment**: Chaotic Good
- **Domains**: Life, Love, Passion
- **Description**: Erosia governs the joyful, free expression of romantic love, artistic passion, and sheer vitality. This deity champions romance, artistic inspiration, and the fierce commitment one feels to a loved one. Followers are encouraged to live life fully and cherish every intense, positive feeling. Erosia believes that true happiness is found in unrestricted feeling and that life is a vibrant tapestry woven from intense experiences. She despises cold calculation and emotional repression.
- **Worshippers**: Lovers, artists, poets, bards, philanthropists, matchmakers, hedonists who seek joy, and anyone who feels life with overwhelming intensity. They honor Erosia through spontaneous acts of generosity, grand gestures of affection, public declarations of love, and the creation of emotionally resonant art.
- **Passive Boon**:
- **Active Boon**: Boon of Unrestrained Vitality Erosia fills you with the overwhelming vitality and courage of passion. You gain Expertise in the Performance skill (doubling your proficiency bonus when making Performance checks). Additionally, once per long rest, you can channel the strength of pure emotion: as a bonus action, you gain advantage on attack rolls against any creature that has recently hurt, threatened, or insulted a person you deeply cherish (including yourself). Furthermore, if you are reduced to 0 hit points but not instantly killed, you are instead reduced to 1 hit point, as the sheer force of your life and love refuses to yield.
