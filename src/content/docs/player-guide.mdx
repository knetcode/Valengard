---

**Refs: https://5e.tools/**

- Take notes
- Ask questions
- Keep track of your character's abilities and inventory
- Feel free to try weird things, this isn't a videogame
- Remember to try and stay in character
- There is no losing or winning
- There will pretty much always be more than one way out of a situation, DM will generally plan for three, but if you can come up with a feasible plan, feel free to try it.
